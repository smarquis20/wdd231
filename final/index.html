Our Mission

RISE is a commonwealth school formed to serve entire families for shared educational and social experiences in a Christ-centered environment based on the restored gospel of Jesus Christ as taught by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

Vision

RISE offers one day of mentored classes every week, as scheduled, from approximately September through April, excluding holiday breaks. Classes are proposed by families in the school and taught by parents, youth, and professionals based on free-market principles. The Well-Educated Heart philosophy is encouraged to support classroom instruction. We seek to preserve a culture of faith, freedom and family, and a love of the good, the true, and the beautiful.
