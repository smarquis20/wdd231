Rise Home Academy Site Plan

Site Name:

risehomeacademy.com

Reason for Selection: This name was chosen because it clearly states the purpose of this site as a home school academy that helps raise their education level. It will provide an academy level experience in the home.

Site Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to provide a centeralized platform for the home school co-op to organize and manage schedules, classes, events, and communcation among members. It will offer tools for collaboration, access to education resources, and a way for families to stay connected to each other and administrators. This site will have features like calendars, member directories, teaching resources, and possibly registration pages.

Scenarios:

I am a parent of a child attending the co-op, and I want to see the schedule of upcoming classes and events?

The parent can visit the Calendar/Events page where they will find a detailed list of classes and events. This page will can be updated and maintained by the administrators.

I'm a new member and I want to access education material and find out more about the co-op?

The new member can visit the Resources page for lesson plans and helpful materials. They can also visit the About Us page to find out more information about the co-op and it's leaders.

Color Schema:

The color scheme will focus on natural, warm colors that are bright and focus on exciting colors that catch your attention.

Primary Color: Purple (#8A2BE2) - Used for the website's headers.

Secondary Color: Turqouise (#40E0D0), Orange (#FF4500), Yellow (#FFD700) - Used for headings, links, and other accents throughout the site.

Typography:

Heading Font: 'Montserrat (Bold)'- Used on Main headings and subheadings across the site to create a clean and easy to read look for titles and sections. descriptions.

Body Font: 'Open Sans'- Used on paragraph text and body content as it is highly readable and friendly.
